# Customer Interview Synthesis, 2026-04-15

Five interviews, each 45 minutes, with champions at existing Nimbus customers. Conducted between 2026-03-20 and 2026-04-10. Delivered to Celeste and Priya on 2026-04-15.

## Headline findings

1. **Acquisition is referral-heavy.** 4 of 5 interviewees cited a specific peer champion who introduced them to Nimbus.
2. **"Operational analytics" language resonates.** All 5 used the term or a close variant to describe their own need, unprompted.
3. **Trial friction is the Salesforce sync.** 3 of 5 named the Salesforce connector setup as the point at which they nearly gave up.
4. **Buyer is revenue ops or head of data, not CMO or CTO.** Zero mentions of marketing or engineering as the buying center.
5. **Comparison set is mostly Hex.** Mixpanel and Amplitude appeared but Hex was named by 3 of 5 as the alternative they seriously evaluated.

## Implications for positioning

- Anchor on "operational analytics" with confidence; customers are already using the frame.
- Referral-heavy acquisition means positioning has to travel well through word of mouth, which argues for a simple, memorable category claim.
- Head-to-head against Hex matters more than against Mixpanel or Amplitude.

# Nimbus Labs Summary

## Who they are

Nimbus Labs is a B2B operational analytics platform for mid-market SaaS companies, founded in Manchester in 2022 (source: internet-research.md). They closed a Series A of $12M in February 2026 led by Notion Capital, bringing headcount to around 45 (source: internet-research.md). Their product sits between traditional BI tools and embedded analytics, giving ops and revenue teams at companies of 100 to 1,000 employees a faster way to stitch product, billing, and CRM data together (source: 2026-03-03_intro-call-transcript.md).

## Why we are working with them

Post Series A, they need to sharpen how they position against a crowded mid-market analytics field (source: 2026-03-03_intro-call-transcript.md). Priya (CEO) wants to defensibly own a category label rather than compete as "yet another dashboard tool." Celeste (VP Marketing) joined in January 2026 and wants external pressure on the positioning work before it calcifies (source: 2026-03-12_positioning-workshop-notes.md).

## What matters to them

- Defensible positioning that supports a Series B narrative 18 months out (source: 2026-03-03_intro-call-transcript.md).
- Clearer ICP so sales stops chasing every SaaS company with more than 100 people (source: 2026-04-15_interview-synthesis.md).
- Turning their product engineering advantage into marketing-legible differentiation. They have real technical depth but struggle to explain it to non-technical buyers (source: 2026-03-12_positioning-workshop-notes.md).

## How they operate

Remote-first, async default. Weekly leadership sync on Mondays, no meetings on Wednesdays. Priya is decisive and values pace; Finn (CTO) is skeptical of marketing spend and will push back on anything that smells like fluff (source: 2026-03-12_positioning-workshop-notes.md). Celeste is our champion and the primary day-to-day contact; she prefers written over verbal recommendations and wants rationale alongside every call (source: 2026-03-03_intro-call-transcript.md).

## What we have learned so far

- Their best customers come from referrals, not marketing (source: 2026-04-15_interview-synthesis.md). Five interviews confirmed this, with four of five citing a specific champion at a peer company.
- The phrase "operational analytics" resonated more than "BI" or "embedded analytics" in both the workshop and customer interviews. Joint call on 2026-03-12 to anchor positioning on that label (see decisions.md).
- Customer concentration is real: top three customers are roughly 40% of ARR (source: 2026-03-03_intro-call-transcript.md). Neither Priya nor Finn flagged this as a risk; worth raising.

## What is changing

- New website launches in late May 2026; messaging draft due to Celeste by 2026-05-10 (source: 2026-03-12_positioning-workshop-notes.md).
- VP of Sales search opened in April 2026. A hire here could shift the engagement priorities materially (source: internet-research.md).

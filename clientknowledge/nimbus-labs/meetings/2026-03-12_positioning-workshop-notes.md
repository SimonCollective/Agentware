# Positioning Workshop, 2026-03-12

## Format

Two-hour session. Five framing candidates tested with the leadership team: "BI for SaaS ops," "Revenue analytics," "Embedded analytics," "Operational intelligence," "Operational analytics."

- Finn pushed back twice on whether we should be doing this before hiring. Priya acknowledged but redirected to the exercise.

- Celeste raised customer concentration in passing (top 3 customers being roughly 40% of ARR). Priya did not engage on this; worth returning to.

## Decisions made in room

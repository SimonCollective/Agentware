Engagement Overview 

  Proposed engagement is a 6-week, “real-time discovery” effort that not
  only maps out Doxim’s detailed pain points and opportunities but
  identifies quick wins immediately, driving measurable ROI from day
  one. By the end of six weeks, Doxim will have a clear AI roadmap,
  visibility into immediate cost savings, all while laying the
  foundation for a sustainable, in-house AI practice.

  Alongside this discovery we will identify 2 MVP projects and build two
  production ready tools to be used by Doxim to drive efficiency into
  the Professional Services and Data teams. These MVPs will be developed
  in parallel to the discovery process, the technology, cost, effort,
  and required platforms will be defined in the first two weeks of
  discovery.

Proposed Approach 

  The engagement will be structured in 2 sections: 

  Section 1

  The “Real-Time Discovery” is a hands-on, iterative approach that
  delivers immediate wins and simultaneously maps a longer-term roadmap.
  By the end of the four weeks, you will have:

1.  Tangible Quick-Win Implementations

-   Identify low-hanging optimization opportunities

    -   A quick win as anything involving pre-existing, pre-configured
        AI technology, for example:

        -   Implementing a better Foundational model for general use

        -   Implementing a better meeting note taker that demonstrates
            significant value over existing solutions

        -   Implementing a marketing or sales tool that has proven value

        -   Implementing automation across engineering or QA that has
            proven value

        -   Training for individuals who are using AI in a sub optimal
            way

2.  AI Roadmap

-   Strategic plan that outlines how Doxim will adopt, implement, and
    scale AI technologies to achieve its business objectives.

3.  Governance & Best Practices

-   A sustainable AI operating model, including compliance guidelines
    for regulated data, recommended training paths for staff, and
    pilot-to-production scale processes.

4.  Financial ROI

-   Clear business-case calculations and KPIs: potential staff reduction
    or capacity reallocation, invoice cycle times, new client throughput
    improvement, etc.

Section 2

1.  Minimum Viable Products (MVP)

-   Analysis and build of opportunities in the following areas;

    -   MVP1 - Professional Services (PS) - Business Analysts (BA),
        Improve Process of Project Document Creation

    -   MVP2 - Data Processing (DP) - Quality Checking in Data
        Processing

-   Technical feasibility outlines, recommended architecture (on-prem
    LLM vs. synthetic data approach for regulated data), timelines, ROI
    for each identified solution.

## 🚀 Overview  
**Real-Time Discovery** is a rapid, hands-on AI enablement engagement designed to uncover, validate, and implement high-impact AI opportunities—*in real time*. In just 4–6 weeks, our team identifies low-effort, high-value wins, initiates MVP development, and delivers an actionable roadmap for long-term AI adoption.

Unlike traditional strategy-first programs, Real-Time Discovery delivers **measurable ROI from week one**—all while building the foundation for a sustainable, in-house AI capability.

# Product Brief: AI Familiarization Day
### Hands-On AI Education and Opportunity Mapping

---

## Overview
The **AI Familiarization Day** is a full-day, in-person workshop designed to bring key stakeholders up to speed on what AI can do today, how to get the most out of it, and where the biggest opportunities are for their specific business.

This is not a generic "AI 101" session. It is tailored to the client's industry, operations, and team composition. By the end of the day, attendees leave with practical AI skills they can use immediately, plus a prioritized list of quick-win use cases specific to their organization.

---

## Who It's For
Organizations that:
- Have team members curious about AI but lacking hands-on experience
- Want to evaluate AI's potential before committing to a larger engagement
- Need a shared baseline of understanding across departments
- Are looking for a low-commitment entry point with immediate value

Works well as:
- A standalone engagement for teams wanting AI literacy
- A kickoff for a broader Real-Time Discovery engagement (replaces Week 1 education)

---

## What the Day Looks Like

### Morning: Foundation and Context (3 hours)
- What AI actually is and what it can realistically do today
- Live demonstrations of leading platforms (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini) and when to use each
- Common misconceptions and pitfalls: why most people get frustrated and how to avoid it
- The art of prompting: how to get consistent, repeatable results

### Afternoon: Applied Workshop (3 hours)
- Hands-on practice with real scenarios relevant to the client's operations
- Fast-paced use case identification exercise tailored to their business
- Prioritization of quick wins: things the team could start doing next week
- Discussion of what a longer engagement would look like (context, not pressure)

---

## Deliverables
- **Tailored Workshop Materials**: Customized for the client's industry and operations
- **Use Case Register**: All opportunities identified during the session, categorized and ranked
- **Quick Win Playbook**: Follow-up document with the top 5 to 10 immediate actions
- **Prompt Library**: Starter prompts relevant to the client's specific workflows

---

## Who Should Attend
Ideal group size: 6 to 12 people. Best results come from a mix of:
- Decision makers and budget holders
- People who do the day-to-day operational work
- Anyone who is curious or enthusiastic about AI
- Potential internal AI champions

---

## Format
- **Duration**: Full day, approximately 6 hours
- **Location**: In-person at the client's office (preferred) or virtual
- **Style**: Interactive, flip chart exercises, live AI demonstrations, group work
- **Preparation**: Pre-session questionnaire to tailor content to the team

---

## Delivery Team
- **Chief AI Officer**: Leads the session, facilitates the workshop
- **Business Analyst / Facilitator**: Supports exercises, captures use cases

---

## Positioning Notes (Internal)
This product serves two purposes:
1. **Standalone value**: Clients get real skills and a concrete action plan
2. **Gateway engagement**: Low commitment entry point that naturally leads to Real-Time Discovery

The Familiarization Day should never feel like a sales pitch for deeper services. It stands on its own. But the afternoon use case exercise naturally surfaces opportunities that a Real-Time Discovery engagement would address, creating organic momentum.
